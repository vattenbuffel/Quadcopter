App used: Arduino Bluetooth controller

Commands:
	Left: 0 : Tilts negatively around x-axis
	Up: 1 : Tilts positively around y-axis
	Right: 2 : Tilts positively around x-axis
	Down: 3 : Tilts negatively around y-axis
	Select: 4 : stop
	Start: 5 : Stops or starts the motors 
	Square: 6 : Heartbeat. If it doesn't receive a heartbeat command or any other command in too long the quad will shutdown. This is to stop the quad from going to far away from the controller
	Triangle: 7 : Increase base_thrust
	Cross: 8 : Unused
	Circle: 9 : Decrease base_thrust
